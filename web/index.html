Attain Moksha With Forgotten Cultures.

Zeus showers his blessings in the form of thunder strikes. Hecate will embody the fest in her magic, Aphrodite will bring out the beauty of life, and Athena will renew a long lost wisdom. Apollo will sharpen the musical scales, and Dionysus will draw upon the drinks and revelry! Moksha will have a new meaning this year.

We invite you to the realm of Moksha to witness a myriad of mythological experiences. Mark your calendars for an exhilarating three days from 29th to 31st March.
